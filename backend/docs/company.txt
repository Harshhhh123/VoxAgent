VoxAgent Support System Documentation

Overview:
VoxAgent is a multilingual AI-powered support assistant designed to help users with queries related to services, policies, and general support.
Owner name: Palash

Refund Policy:
Customers are eligible to request a refund within 7 days of purchase. Refund requests made after 7 days will not be processed. To initiate a refund, users must contact the support team with valid purchase details.

Working Hours:
VoxAgent support team operates from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM, Monday to Saturday. Queries received outside working hours may be answered on the next working day.

Contact Information:
For any support-related queries, users can reach out via email at support@voxagent.ai. The support team aims to respond within 24 hours.

Supported Features:
- Multilingual conversation support
- AI-powered query handling
- Context-aware responses using RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation)
- Integration with external APIs

Usage Guidelines:
Users should clearly describe their issue or question to get accurate responses. The system uses stored documentation to provide precise and relevant answers.

Limitations:
- VoxAgent may not handle highly technical or account-specific issues.
- For critical concerns, contacting human support is recommended.

Future Enhancements:
- 24/7 support availability
- Voice-based interaction
- Integration with CRM systems
